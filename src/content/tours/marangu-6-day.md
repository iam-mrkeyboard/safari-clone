The extended 6-day Marangu route includes an additional acclimatization day for higher summit success. This itinerary features an overnight at Horombo Hut with a hike to Mawenzi Ridge, plus an extra night on the descent for a more comfortable pace. Hut accommodation throughout makes this a popular choice for those seeking comfort and safety.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Marangu Gate – Mandara Hut

Drive from Moshi to Marangu Gate. After registration, trek through lush rainforest to Mandara Hut (2,700m). The trail is shaded and cool, passing beneath giant trees draped with moss. Afternoon optional walk to Maundi Crater.

**Highlights:** Rainforest trek with colobus monkeys and Maundi Crater views.

**Accommodation:** Mandara Hut
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Mandara Hut – Horombo Hut

Ascend through montane forest into heath and moorland. The trail opens to spectacular views of Kibo and Mawenzi. Arrive at Horombo Hut (3,720m). Afternoon acclimatization walk recommended.

**Highlights:** First dramatic views of Kilimanjaro's snow-capped peaks.

**Accommodation:** Horombo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Horombo Hut – Acclimatization Day

Acclimatization day at Horombo. Hike to Mawenzi Ridge (4,300m) for incredible views of the jagged Mawenzi peak and the vast alpine desert beyond. This extra day significantly increases summit success rates.

**Highlights:** Hike to Mawenzi Ridge with panoramic high-altitude views.

**Accommodation:** Horombo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Horombo Hut – Kibo Hut

Trek across the saddle between Kibo and Mawenzi to Kibo Hut (4,703m). The landscape is a stark alpine desert. Arrive early for rest and preparation for the midnight summit attempt.

**Highlights:** Crossing the lunar-like saddle beneath Kilimanjaro's massive bulk.

**Accommodation:** Kibo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Summit Day – Descent to Horombo

Depart at midnight for the summit. Reach Gilman's Point at sunrise (5,681m) on the crater rim, then continue to Uhuru Peak (5,895m). Celebrate at the Roof of Africa. Descend to Horombo Hut for overnight.

**Highlights:** Sunrise at Africa's highest point, the ultimate achievement.

**Accommodation:** Horombo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Horombo Hut – Marangu Gate – Moshi

Descend through the rainforest to Marangu Gate. Receive your summit certificate and transfer to Moshi.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch
