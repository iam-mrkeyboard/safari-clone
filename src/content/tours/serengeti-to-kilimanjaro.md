An epic 14-day journey combining Tanzania's greatest highlights: wildlife safaris in the Serengeti and Ngorongoro, a trek on Mount Kilimanjaro, and cultural encounters. This comprehensive adventure is designed for those who want the complete Tanzanian experience from the savannah to the summit.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport. Transfer to your Arusha lodge. Evening briefing with your guide about the incredible journey ahead.

**Highlights:** Arrival and welcome in the safari capital of Tanzania.

**Accommodation:** Arusha Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Full-day game drive in Tarangire National Park. Known for its massive baobab trees, large elephant herds, and prolific birdlife. Picnic lunch in the bush. Evening transfer to your lodge.

**Highlights:** Elephant sightings among iconic baobab landscapes.

**Accommodation:** Tarangire Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Tarangire – Lake Manyara – Karatu

Morning game drive, then visit Lake Manyara National Park. Famous for tree-climbing lions, flamingos, and groundwater forest. Continue to the Ngorongoro highlands for overnight.

**Highlights:** Tree-climbing lions and diverse birdlife at Lake Manyara.

**Accommodation:** Karatu Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Tour

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a half-day game drive. This UNESCO site offers extraordinary wildlife density. Spot lions, elephants, black rhino, hippos, and more. Picnic lunch on the crater floor. Afternoon at leisure at your rim lodge.

**Highlights:** Ngorongoro Crater, Africa's Garden of Eden.

**Accommodation:** Ngorongoro Rim Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Ngorongoro – Serengeti National Park

Drive into the Serengeti via the beautiful highlands. Game drive en route to your camp. The central Serengeti (Seronera) offers year-round excellent game viewing with high predator densities.

**Highlights:** Arrival in the world-famous Serengeti plains.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Full Day Serengeti Game Drive

Full day exploring the Serengeti ecosystem. Your guide will search for the Great Migration herds, big cats, and the diverse wildlife that calls these plains home. Bush breakfast and picnic lunch included.

**Highlights:** An immersive day in Africa's greatest wildlife reserve.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 7: Serengeti – Arusha

Morning game drive, then fly or drive back to Arusha. Afternoon at leisure. Prepare for your Kilimanjaro trek briefing with your mountain guide.

**Highlights:** Scenic journey back to Arusha with safari memories.

**Accommodation:** Arusha Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 8: Arusha – Kilimanjaro Trek Start

Transfer to the Kilimanjaro trailhead. Depending on your chosen route (Marangu, Machame, or Lemosho), begin your trek. This day marks the start of your ascent to Africa's highest peak.

**Highlights:** Beginning your Kilimanjaro climbing adventure.

**Accommodation:** Mountain Hut/Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 9: Kilimanjaro Trek Day 2

Continue ascending through montane forest and heath zones. Each day brings new landscapes and ever-expanding views. Proper acclimatization and pacing are emphasized.

**Highlights:** Climbing through Kilimanjaro's diverse climate zones.

**Accommodation:** Mountain Hut/Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 10: Kilimanjaro Trek Day 3

Trek through alpine desert and moorland. The vegetation thins as you gain altitude. Dramatic views of the glaciers and crater rim ahead.

**Highlights:** Alpine desert landscapes with glacier views.

**Accommodation:** Mountain Hut/Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 11: Summit Day

Summit attempt begins around midnight. Reach the crater rim at sunrise (Stella Point or Gilman's Point). Continue to Uhuru Peak (5,895m), the highest point in Africa. Descend to a lower camp for overnight.

**Highlights:** Sunrise at Uhuru Peak, the rooftop of Africa.

**Accommodation:** Mountain Hut/Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 12: Descent

Complete the descent to the park gate. Receive your summit certificate. Transfer to your Moshi or Arusha lodge. Celebration dinner.

**Highlights:** Celebrating your Kilimanjaro summit achievement.

**Accommodation:** Arusha Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 13: Arusha – Lake Duluti & Cultural Tour

Relaxed day exploring Arusha. Visit Lake Duluti for a nature walk and canoeing. Tour a local coffee plantation or cultural center. Farewell dinner with traditional Tanzanian cuisine and entertainment.

**Highlights:** Relaxed cultural immersion on your final full day.

**Accommodation:** Arusha Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 14: Departure

Transfer to Kilimanjaro International Airport for departure.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
