The Wonders Gate Safari is a comprehensive 6-day journey through Tanzania's most iconic parks. Starting in Tarangire, crossing the Serengeti, and descending into Ngorongoro Crater, this safari offers an immersive wildlife experience. Visit the remote gates and lesser-known corners of each park for a truly unique adventure away from the crowds.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Depart Arusha and drive to Tarangire National Park. Known for its ancient baobab trees and large elephant herds, Tarangire offers excellent game viewing. Enjoy a full afternoon game drive, spotting elephants, lions, giraffes, and the park's famous tree-climbing pythons. Dinner and overnight at your lodge near the park.

**Highlights:** Game drive among iconic baobab-studded landscapes with massive elephant herds.

**Accommodation:** Tarangire Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Tarangire – Lake Manyara – Karatu

Morning game drive in Tarangire. After lunch, drive to Lake Manyara National Park for an afternoon game drive. Famous for its tree-climbing lions, flamingos, and lush groundwater forest, Lake Manyara offers a compact but rewarding safari experience. Continue to Karatu for overnight.

**Highlights:** Tree-climbing lions and flamingo-lined shores of Lake Manyara.

**Accommodation:** Karatu Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Karatu – Serengeti National Park

After breakfast, drive through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area to the Serengeti. Descend into the endless plains for your first game drive en route to your camp. The central Serengeti (Seronera) is famous for its high predator density. Check in and enjoy dinner under the stars.

**Highlights:** Arrival in the Serengeti with game viewing en route.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Full Day Serengeti Game Drive

A full day exploring the Serengeti ecosystem. Your expert guide will track wildlife movements, following the Great Migration or locating big cats in their territory. Visit the Seronera River Valley, known for its resident lion prides and leopards. Picnic lunch in the bush. Optional hot air balloon safari at sunrise.

**Highlights:** Full day game drive in Africa's most famous wildlife sanctuary.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater

Morning game drive en route to Ngorongoro. Descend into the caldera for a half-day game drive, exploring the crater floor's diverse habitats. Spot lions, elephants, buffalo, hippos, and the endangered black rhino. Picnic lunch by the hippo pool. Ascent and drive to your crater rim lodge.

**Highlights:** Ngorongoro Crater game drive with incredible Big Five density.

**Accommodation:** Ngorongoro Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Ngorongoro – Arusha

Enjoy breakfast overlooking the crater. Visit a local Maasai village for a cultural experience before driving back to Arusha. Arrive by early afternoon for your onward travel.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch
