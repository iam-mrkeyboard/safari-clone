The Machame Route, known as the Whiskey Route, is one of Kilimanjaro's most scenic and popular trails. This 6-day camping trek offers stunning biodiversity, dramatic landscapes, and excellent acclimatization through the climb-high, sleep-low philosophy. It demands a bit more stamina but rewards with incredible variety and higher summit success.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Machame Gate – Machame Camp

Drive from Moshi to Machame Gate (1,800m). Trek through lush montane rainforest on a steep, scenic trail. The forest is alive with birds, monkeys, and wildflowers. Arrive at Machame Camp (3,010m) for your first night under canvas.

**Highlights:** Steep rainforest trek with rich biodiversity and birdlife.

**Accommodation:** Machame Camp
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Machame Camp – Shira Camp

Leave the forest behind as you enter the heath and moorland zone. The trail climbs through giant heather and volcanic rock formations. Arrive at Shira Camp (3,840m) with stunning views of Mount Meru and the Shira Plateau. Afternoon acclimatization walk.

**Highlights:** Emerging from the forest onto the vast Shira Plateau.

**Accommodation:** Shira Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Shira Camp – Barranco Camp

Ascend through the alpine desert, passing the Lava Tower (4,630m) for acclimatization. Descend into the beautiful Barranco Valley, arriving at Barranco Camp (3,960m). The descent helps your body adjust. Spectacular views of the Breach Wall and Heim Glacier.

**Highlights:** The climb-high, sleep-low principle at Lava Tower and Barranco Valley.

**Accommodation:** Barranco Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Barranco Camp – Karanga Camp

Tackle the famous Barranco Wall, a steep but non-technical scramble. The views from the top are breathtaking. Traverse through the Karanga Valley, crossing rivers and ridges. Arrive at Karanga Camp (4,035m) for overnight.

**Highlights:** Conquering the dramatic Barranco Wall with panoramic views.

**Accommodation:** Karanga Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Karanga Camp – Barafu Camp

Continue ascending through alpine desert to Barafu Camp (4,673m). This is your base camp for the summit attempt. Set up camp, rest, and prepare gear. Early dinner and sleep before the midnight summit push.

**Highlights:** Final camp before the summit, with views of the peak ahead.

**Accommodation:** Barafu Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Summit Day – Descent to Mweka

Depart at midnight for the summit. Climb by headlamp through scree to Stella Point (5,756m), then continue to Uhuru Peak (5,895m) at sunrise. Descend via the Mweka Route to Mweka Camp (3,100m) for your final night.

**Highlights:** Sunrise at Uhuru Peak, the highest point on the African continent.

**Accommodation:** Mweka Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
