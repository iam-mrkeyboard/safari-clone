Discover the magic of Zanzibar on this 5-day adventure that combines culture, history, spice, and pristine beaches. From the labyrinthine streets of Stone Town to the powder-white sands of the east coast, this tour offers the perfect blend of exploration and relaxation on the Spice Island.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival – Stone Town

Arrive at Zanzibar Airport and transfer to your Stone Town hotel. Afternoon guided walking tour through the UNESCO-listed old town, visiting the House of Wonders, Old Fort, and the lively Forodhani Gardens food market. Evening sunset dhow cruise along the harbor.

**Highlights:** Stone Town exploration and sunset dhow cruise.

**Accommodation:** Stone Town Hotel  
**Meal Plan:** Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Spice Tour – Beach Transfer

Morning spice plantation tour where you'll discover why Zanzibar is called the Spice Island. See and taste cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, vanilla, and more. Afternoon transfer to your beach resort on the east or north coast. Rest of the day at leisure.

**Highlights:** Sensory spice tour followed by beach resort check-in.

**Accommodation:** Beach Resort  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Mnemba Island Snorkeling

Full-day excursion to Mnemba Island, a private atoll surrounded by a protected marine reserve. Snorkel among vibrant coral reefs teeming with tropical fish, sea turtles, and dolphins. Enjoy a freshly prepared seafood lunch on the boat or on the beach. Return to your resort late afternoon.

**Highlights:** World-class snorkeling at Mnemba Atoll marine reserve.

**Accommodation:** Beach Resort  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Jozani Forest & Beach Relaxation

Morning visit to Jozani Forest, Zanzibar's only national park, home to the rare Red Colobus monkey. Walk through the ancient groundwater forest on elevated boardwalks. Afternoon free to relax on the beach, swim, or enjoy water sports.

**Highlights:** Encounter Zanzibar's rare Red Colobus monkeys in their natural habitat.

**Accommodation:** Beach Resort  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Departure

Breakfast at the resort. Transfer to Zanzibar International Airport for your departure.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
