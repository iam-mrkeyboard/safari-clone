Mount Meru (4,566m) offers one of Africa's most beautiful and underrated treks. This 4-day itinerary includes an extra acclimatization day for better summit success. The route traverses lush rainforest, giant heather moorlands, and volcanic ash slopes, culminating in a sunrise summit with breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arusha – Momella Gate – Miriakamba Hut

Drive from Arusha to Momella Gate. After registration, trek through dense montane forest alive with bird calls and colobus monkeys. The trail climbs steadily to Miriakamba Hut (2,500m). Afternoon short walk for views of the Meru Crater and Kilimanjaro.

**Highlights:** Forest trek with wildlife sightings and stunning views towards Kilimanjaro.

**Accommodation:** Miriakamba Hut  
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Miriakamba Hut – Saddle Hut

Climb through the heath and moorland zone with giant lobelias and groundsels. The trail offers dramatic views of the crater wall. Arrive at Saddle Hut (3,570m) for lunch. Afternoon acclimatization walk to Little Meru Peak (3,820m) for panoramic sunset views.

**Highlights:** Alpine zone with giant groundsels, sunset from Little Meru Peak.

**Accommodation:** Saddle Hut  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Saddle Hut – Summit – Miriakamba Hut

Early morning start (midnight) for the summit attempt. Climb by headlamp through rocky terrain to the crater rim, then follow the ridge to Socialist Peak (4,566m). Witness a spectacular sunrise over Kilimanjaro. Descend to Saddle Hut for a brief rest, then continue to Miriakamba Hut for the night.

**Highlights:** Sunrise summit at 4,566m with Kilimanjaro backdrop.

**Accommodation:** Miriakamba Hut  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Miriakamba Hut – Momella Gate – Arusha

Descend through the forest to Momella Gate. Receive your summit certificate. Transfer back to Arusha, arriving by early afternoon.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch
