The ultimate Tanzania experience combining world-class safari with tropical beach bliss. This 11-day luxury journey takes you from the vast Serengeti plains to the pristine beaches of Zanzibar. Experience the Great Migration, explore the Ngorongoro Crater, and unwind on powder-white sand beaches in perfect harmony.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport. Your private guide will meet you and transfer you to your Arusha lodge. Evening briefing with your safari manager. Relax and prepare for your adventure.

**Highlights:** Arrival and welcome at a luxury Arusha lodge.

**Accommodation:** Luxury Arusha Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Drive to Tarangire National Park for a full-day game drive. Tarangire's baobab-dotted landscape is home to the largest elephant herds in Tanzania. Enjoy a bush picnic lunch. Late afternoon, drive to your luxury camp or lodge.

**Highlights:** Full day in Tarangire with spectacular elephant sightings.

**Accommodation:** Luxury Tarangire Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Tarangire – Lake Manyara – Ngorongoro Highlands

Morning game drive in Tarangire. After lunch, visit Lake Manyara for a game drive featuring tree-climbing lions and vast flamingo flocks. Continue to your lodge perched on the Ngorongoro Crater rim with breathtaking views.

**Highlights:** Lake Manyara's unique wildlife and Ngorongoro rim sunset.

**Accommodation:** Ngorongoro Rim Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Tour

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a full-day game drive. This UNESCO World Heritage Site offers the highest density of wildlife in Africa. Spot the Big Five, including the endangered black rhino. Picnic lunch by the hippo pool. Return to your rim lodge for dinner.

**Highlights:** Full-day exploration of the world's largest intact volcanic caldera.

**Accommodation:** Ngorongoro Rim Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Ngorongoro – Serengeti National Park

Drive through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area into the Serengeti. Game drive en route to your luxury camp. The central Serengeti offers exceptional predator viewing. Welcome sundowners and dinner under the vast African sky.

**Highlights:** Arrival in the Serengeti with game viewing along the way.

**Accommodation:** Luxury Serengeti Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Full Day Serengeti Game Drive

A full day exploring the Serengeti ecosystem. Your guide will track the Great Migration or locate big cats. Visit remote areas away from other vehicles for exclusive game viewing. Bush breakfast, picnic lunch, and sunset sundowners included.

**Highlights:** Exclusive and immersive full-day Serengeti safari experience.

**Accommodation:** Luxury Serengeti Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 7: Serengeti – Flight to Zanzibar

Morning game drive, then fly from the Serengeti airstrip to Zanzibar. Upon arrival, transfer to your beach resort. The rest of the day is yours to relax by the pool, walk the beach, or enjoy spa treatments.

**Highlights:** Scenic flight from the savannah to the tropical archipelago.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 8: Zanzibar Beach Relaxation

A full day at leisure on Zanzibar's stunning beaches. Swim in turquoise waters, sunbathe on white sand, or join optional excursions including snorkeling at Mnemba Island, spice tours, or Stone Town exploration.

**Highlights:** Complete relaxation on Zanzibar's world-famous beaches.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 9: Spice Tour & Stone Town

Morning spice plantation tour exploring Zanzibar's fragrant spice farms. Afternoon guided tour of historic Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Evening dhow cruise at sunset with sparkling wine and canapes.

**Highlights:** Cultural immersion exploring Zanzibar's spice heritage and history.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 10: Full Day Beach Leisure

Your final full day in paradise. Choose from water sports, a spa day, snorkeling excursions, or simply relax under the tropical sun. Farewell dinner at the resort's finest restaurant.

**Highlights:** A final day of tropical indulgence.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 11: Departure

Breakfast and transfer to Zanzibar International Airport for departure.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
