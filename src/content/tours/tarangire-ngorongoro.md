A perfect short safari for those with limited time. Experience the baobab-studded landscapes of Tarangire and the wildlife-dense Ngorongoro Crater in just two days. This compact adventure delivers incredible game viewing and a taste of Tanzania's most famous parks.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Tarangire National Park

Depart Arusha after breakfast and drive to Tarangire National Park. Known for its massive baobab trees and large elephant herds, Tarangire offers a unique safari experience. Enjoy a full game drive with a picnic lunch in the park, spotting elephants, giraffes, zebras, and if lucky, lions lounging in the shade. Late afternoon, drive to your lodge near Karatu or Ngorongoro for dinner and overnight.

**Highlights:** Game viewing among ancient baobab trees, large elephant herds, and prolific birdlife.

**Accommodation:** Lodge near Ngorongoro  
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Ngorongoro Crater Tour

After an early breakfast, descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a half-day game drive. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is one of the best places in Africa to see the Big Five. The crater floor teems with lions, elephants, buffalo, hippos, and an astonishing density of wildlife. Enjoy a picnic lunch by the hippo pool before ascending the crater wall and driving back to Arusha.

**Highlights:** Descend into the world's largest intact volcanic caldera and witness extraordinary wildlife concentrations.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch
