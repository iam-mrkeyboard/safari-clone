The Lemosho Route is considered the most scenic path up Kilimanjaro, offering panoramic views, diverse ecosystems, and excellent acclimatization. This 7-day itinerary crosses the untouched western side of the mountain, traverses the vast Shira Plateau, and joins the Machame Route for the final ascent. Low crowds and high success rates make it a top choice.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Londorossi Gate – Mti Mkubwa Camp

Drive to Londorossi Gate for registration. Trek through pristine montane rainforest on a quiet trail. The forest is home to colobus monkeys, buffalo, and丰富的 birdlife. Arrive at Mti Mkubwa Camp (2,820m), meaning Big Tree Camp.

**Highlights:** Remote start through pristine rainforest with abundant wildlife.

**Accommodation:** Mti Mkubwa Camp
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Mti Mkubwa Camp – Shira Camp 1

Continue ascending through increasingly sparse forest. Emerge onto the Shira Plateau, a high-altitude volcanic desert. The views are breathtaking with Mount Meru to the east and Kilimanjaro's glaciers ahead. Arrive at Shira Camp 1 (3,500m).

**Highlights:** Emerging from the forest onto the vast, dramatic Shira Plateau.

**Accommodation:** Shira Camp 1
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Shira Camp 1 – Shira Camp 2

A gentle day traversing the Shira Plateau. The landscape is otherworldly with volcanic rock formations and endemic giant groundsels. Arrive at Shira Camp 2 (3,850m) for lunch. Afternoon acclimatization walk to Cathedral Point for stunning views.

**Highlights:** Exploring the wild, windswept beauty of the Shira Plateau.

**Accommodation:** Shira Camp 2
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Shira Camp 2 – Lava Tower – Barranco Camp

Ascend to Lava Tower (4,630m) for lunch, then descend to Barranco Camp (3,960m) following the climb-high, sleep-low principle. The Barranco Valley is lush and green, with dramatic views of the Breach Wall and Heim Glacier.

**Highlights:** Lava Tower acclimatization and the beautiful Barranco Valley.

**Accommodation:** Barranco Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Barranco Camp – Karanga Camp

Climb the famous Barranco Wall, a non-technical but exhilarating scramble. Continue through the Karanga Valley to Karanga Camp (4,035m). The views of the southern ice fields are spectacular. Afternoon rest.

**Highlights:** The iconic Barranco Wall scramble with glacier views.

**Accommodation:** Karanga Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Karanga Camp – Barafu Camp

Ascend through alpine desert to Barafu Camp (4,673m). The landscape is stark and lunar, with Kilimanjaro's summit looming above. Prepare your gear, rest, and eat an early dinner before the midnight summit attempt.

**Highlights:** Base camp beneath Kilimanjaro's summit with sunset views.

**Accommodation:** Barafu Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 7: Summit Day – Descent to Mweka

Depart at midnight. Climb by headlamp to Stella Point (5,756m) at sunrise, then continue to Uhuru Peak (5,895m). Descend via the Mweka Route to Mweka Camp (3,100m) for your final night.

**Highlights:** Sunrise at Uhuru Peak, Africa's highest point.

**Accommodation:** Mweka Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
