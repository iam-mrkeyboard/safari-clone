Mount Meru, Tanzania's second-highest peak at 4,566 meters, offers a thrilling trek through diverse ecosystems and stunning views. Often used as an acclimatization climb for Kilimanjaro, this 3-day adventure takes you through lush rainforest, alpine moorland, and rocky ridges to a summit that rewards you with panoramic vistas of Kilimanjaro and the Great Rift Valley.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arusha – Momella Gate – Miriakamba Hut

Depart Arusha and drive to Momella Gate for park registration. Begin your trek through dense montane forest, home to colobus monkeys, giraffes, and buffalo. The trail climbs steadily to Miriakamba Hut (2,500m) where you'll overnight. The afternoon offers excellent views of the Meru Crater and Mount Kilimanjaro in the distance.

**Highlights:** Forest trek with wildlife sightings and first views of the summit.

**Accommodation:** Miriakamba Hut  
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Miriakamba Hut – Saddle Hut

Today's trek is shorter but steeper as you climb through heath and moorland zones to Saddle Hut (3,570m). The landscape transforms into alpine desert with giant lobelias and groundsels. Afternoon acclimatization walk to Little Meru (3,820m) for spectacular sunset views over the crater.

**Highlights:** Alpine landscapes, giant lobelias, and sunset at Little Meru peak.

**Accommodation:** Saddle Hut  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Summiting – Descent to Arusha

Start your summit attempt at midnight, climbing by headlamp through steep, rocky terrain. Reach Socialist Peak (4,566m) at sunrise for breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro and the surrounding plains. Descend back to Saddle Hut for a short rest, then continue down to Momella Gate. Transfer back to Arusha.

**Highlights:** Sunrise at 4,566m with panoramic views of Kilimanjaro and the Great Rift Valley.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch
