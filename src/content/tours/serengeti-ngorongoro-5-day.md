An exceptional mid-range safari that covers Tanzania's two most iconic destinations. Spend two full days exploring the endless plains of the Serengeti in search of the Great Migration and big cats, then descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for world-class game viewing. Comfortable lodges and expert guiding make this a superb-value adventure.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arusha – Lake Manyara – Karatu

Depart Arusha and drive to Lake Manyara National Park for a game drive. Famous for its tree-climbing lions and vast flocks of flamingos, Lake Manyara offers a beautiful introduction to Tanzania's wildlife. Afternoon drive to Karatu for overnight.

**Highlights:** Tree-climbing lions and flamingo-lined lake shores.

**Accommodation:** Karatu Lodge  
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Karatu – Serengeti National Park

After breakfast, drive through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area to the Serengeti. Enjoy game viewing en route as you descend into the plains. Afternoon game drive in the central Serengeti (Seronera area), known for its high concentration of predators. Dinner and overnight at your camp.

**Highlights:** Arrival in the Serengeti with afternoon predator-spotting.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Camp  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Full Day Serengeti Game Drive

A full day exploring the Serengeti ecosystem. Your guide will track the movement of wildlife, following the Great Migration herds or locating big cats depending on the season. Picnic lunch in the bush maximizes your game viewing time. Return to camp for dinner.

**Highlights:** Full day immersion in Africa's greatest wildlife spectacle.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Camp  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater – Karatu

Morning game drive en route to Ngorongoro. Descend into the crater for a half-day safari through this UNESCO World Heritage Site. Spot lions, elephants, buffalo, hippos, and the endangered black rhino. Picnic lunch by the hippo pool. Ascend and drive to Karatu for overnight.

**Highlights:** Ngorongoro Crater game drive with incredible wildlife density.

**Accommodation:** Karatu Lodge  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Departure

Breakfast and drive back to Arusha. Arrive by midday for your onward flight.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
