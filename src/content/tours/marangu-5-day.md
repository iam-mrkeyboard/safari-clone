The Marangu Route, also known as the Coca-Cola Route, is the most established and accessible path up Kilimanjaro. This 5-day trek follows a gentle gradient through lush rainforest to the snow-capped summit, offering hut accommodation throughout. It's the only route with sleeping huts, making it ideal for those who prefer not to camp.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Marangu Gate – Mandara Hut

Drive from Moshi to Marangu Gate (1,870m). After registration, trek through dense rainforest to Mandara Hut (2,700m). The trail is shaded and lush, with colobus monkeys and diverse birdlife. Afternoon short walk to Maundi Crater for views of the Kenyan border.

**Highlights:** Rainforest trek with rich birdlife and views from Maundi Crater.

**Accommodation:** Mandara Hut
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Mandara Hut – Horombo Hut

Trek through montane forest that gradually gives way to heath and moorland. The trail opens to stunning views of Kibo and Mawenzi peaks. Arrive at Horombo Hut (3,720m) for lunch. Afternoon acclimatization walk recommended.

**Highlights:** Emerging from the forest with first views of the twin peaks.

**Accommodation:** Horombo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Horombo Hut – Acclimatization Day

An essential acclimatization day. Morning hike to Zebra Rocks (4,100m) for panoramic views, then return to Horombo. This extra day dramatically improves summit success rates by helping your body adjust to the altitude.

**Highlights:** Acclimatization walk to Zebra Rocks with breathtaking mountain panoramas.

**Accommodation:** Horombo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Horombo Hut – Kibo Hut

Continue through the lunar-like alpine desert to Kibo Hut (4,703m). The trail passes the saddle between Kibo and Mawenzi. Arrive early for rest and prepare for the midnight summit attempt. Early dinner and sleep.

**Highlights:** Crossing the alpine desert saddle between Kilimanjaro's twin peaks.

**Accommodation:** Kibo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Summit Day – Descent to Horombo

Wake at midnight for the summit attempt. Climb by headlamp through switchbacks to Gilman's Point (5,681m) on the crater rim. Continue along the rim to Uhuru Peak (5,895m). Witness an unforgettable sunrise. Descend to Horombo Hut for overnight.

**Highlights:** Sunrise at Uhuru Peak, the rooftop of Africa at 5,895 meters.

**Accommodation:** Horombo Hut
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
