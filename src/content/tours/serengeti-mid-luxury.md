This safari is designed to give you the ultimate Tanzanian experience with enough time in both Serengeti and Ngorongoro. Starting with a night in Karatu to break up the journey, you'll then spend two days in Serengeti, the heart of African wildlife, before exploring the legendary Ngorongoro Crater. With handpicked lodges, private guiding, and seamless planning, this journey balances adventure with comfort.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Arusha – Transfer to Karatu

Arrive at Arusha (ARK/KIA) where your private Tazama guide will meet you. From there, you'll be driven to Karatu to rest and prepare for your safari. This stop makes the journey to Serengeti easier and gives you a relaxed start after your flight.

**Highlights:** A comfortable overnight in Karatu so you can start your Serengeti adventure fresh the next morning.

**Accommodation:** Acacia Farm Lodge  
**Meal Plan:** Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Karatu to Serengeti National Park

After an early breakfast, begin the scenic drive toward Serengeti. Pass through Ngorongoro highlands before descending into the endless plains, with wildlife viewing along the way. Arrive in the Central Serengeti for your first game drive and settle into your camp.

**Highlights:** Scenic journey and your first safari moments in the Serengeti.

**Accommodation:** Nyikani Central Serengeti  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Full Day Serengeti Game Drive

Spend the day exploring Serengeti with your guide. Whether following big cats around the kopje or watching elephants cross the plains, each moment in Serengeti is unforgettable. Picnic lunch is carried to maximize your time in the park.

**Highlights:** A full day immersed in Africa's most iconic safari destination.

**Accommodation:** Nyikani Central Serengeti  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater – Karatu

After breakfast, depart Serengeti and head toward Ngorongoro. Descend into the crater for a half-day game drive, a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its dense wildlife population. Later, continue to Karatu for your overnight stay.

**Highlights:** Explore the Ngorongoro Crater, one of the best places to see wildlife in a single day.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a relaxed breakfast before your guide transfers you to the airport for your onward flight.

**Highlights:** A smooth end to your safari with lasting memories of Serengeti and Ngorongoro.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
