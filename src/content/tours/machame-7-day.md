The 7-day Machame Route offers the best summit success rates on Kilimanjaro thanks to an extra acclimatization day. This camping trek traverses five distinct climate zones, from rainforest to arctic summit, and includes a full extra day at Karanga Camp for optimal altitude adjustment. The most recommended itinerary for serious trekkers.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Machame Gate – Machame Camp

Drive to Machame Gate and begin your trek through dense montane rainforest. The trail climbs steadily through moss-draped trees and colorful impatiens flowers. Arrive at Machame Camp (3,010m) for dinner and overnight.

**Highlights:** Beautiful rainforest trek with abundant birdlife and wildflowers.

**Accommodation:** Machame Camp
**Meal Plan:** Lunch, Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Machame Camp – Shira Camp

Exit the forest into heath and moorland. The trail climbs through volcanic terrain with giant heather and protea plants. Arrive at Shira Camp (3,840m) with panoramic views of the Shira Plateau and Mount Meru. Afternoon acclimatization walk recommended.

**Highlights:** Transition from forest to the vast Shira Plateau.

**Accommodation:** Shira Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Shira Camp – Lava Tower – Barranco Camp

Ascend to Lava Tower (4,630m) for lunch and acclimatization, then descend into the lush Barranco Valley. This climb-high, sleep-low day is crucial for altitude adjustment. Barranco Camp (3,960m) sits beneath the towering Breach Wall.

**Highlights:** Lava Tower acclimatization and the stunning Barranco Valley.

**Accommodation:** Barranco Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Barranco Camp – Karanga Camp

Scale the dramatic Barranco Wall, a thrilling scramble with incredible views. Traverse the Karanga Valley with its undulating ridges and streams. Arrive at Karanga Camp (4,035m) for a relaxed afternoon and early night.

**Highlights:** Conquering the Barranco Wall, one of the route's most exciting sections.

**Accommodation:** Karanga Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Karanga Camp – Acclimatization Day

A full acclimatization day at Karanga. Hike to higher elevations on the slopes of Kilimanjaro and return to camp. This extra day dramatically improves your summit prospects. Rest, hydrate, and prepare for the summit push.

**Highlights:** Dedicated acclimatization for maximum summit success.

**Accommodation:** Karanga Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Karanga Camp – Barafu Camp

Ascend through alpine desert to Barafu Camp (4,673m). The landscape is stark and lunar. Prepare your gear and rest. Early dinner and lights out by 19:00 for the midnight summit attempt.

**Highlights:** Final camp preparation before the summit bid.

**Accommodation:** Barafu Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 7: Summit Day – Descent to Mweka

Depart at midnight for the summit. Reach Stella Point (5,756m) at sunrise, then continue along the crater rim to Uhuru Peak (5,895m), the highest point in Africa. Descend via the Mweka Route to Mweka Camp (3,100m) for your final night.

**Highlights:** Sunrise at Uhuru Peak, the ultimate Kilimanjaro achievement.

**Accommodation:** Mweka Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
