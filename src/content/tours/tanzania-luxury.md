The pinnacle of African luxury travel. This 14-day journey combines the finest safari camps in Tanzania's most exclusive wildlife areas with the ultimate beachfront villa experience in Zanzibar. Private guides, fly-in transfers, gourmet dining, and unparalleled service define this extraordinary itinerary.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport. VIP meet-and-greet and private transfer to a luxury Arusha lodge. Champagne welcome. Evening briefing with your private safari consultant.

**Highlights:** VIP arrival and welcome at a world-class lodge.

**Accommodation:** Arusha Luxury Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Arusha – Lake Manyara – Ngorongoro Crater

Private guided tour of Lake Manyara National Park with its famous tree-climbing lions. Picnic champagne lunch. Continue to the Ngorongoro Crater rim for sunset cocktails overlooking the caldera.

**Highlights:** Sunset cocktails on the Ngorongoro Crater rim.

**Accommodation:** Ngorongoro Luxury Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater Tour

Private full-day game drive in Ngorongoro Crater. Descend into the caldera with your expert guide for unrivaled wildlife viewing. Gourmet picnic lunch by the hippo pool. Return to your lodge for spa treatments and dinner.

**Highlights:** Private game drive in Africa's most spectacular natural arena.

**Accommodation:** Ngorongoro Luxury Lodge
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Ngorongoro – Serengeti National Park

Scenic drive to the Serengeti with game viewing en route. Check into your luxury camp. Afternoon private game drive followed by sundowners in the bush and a gourmet dinner under the stars.

**Highlights:** Arrival at your luxury Serengeti camp with bush sundowners.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Luxury Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Full Day Serengeti Game Drive

Full-day private game drive exploring the Serengeti ecosystem. Follow the Great Migration or track big cats with your dedicated guide. Bush breakfast, gourmet picnic lunch, and sunset cocktails included.

**Highlights:** Private safari with exclusive access to prime wildlife areas.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Luxury Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Hot Air Balloon Safari & Game Drive

Sunrise hot air balloon safari over the Serengeti plains. Champagne bush breakfast after landing. Midday rest at camp. Afternoon game drive exploring different areas of the Serengeti.

**Highlights:** Balloon safari over the Serengeti with champagne breakfast.

**Accommodation:** Serengeti Luxury Camp
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 7: Serengeti – Fly to Zanzibar

Morning private game drive. Fly from the Serengeti airstrip to Zanzibar. Private transfer to your exclusive beachfront villa. Welcome champagne and canapes. Evening at leisure.

**Highlights:** Private flight from the savannah to paradise.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Luxury Villa
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 8: Zanzibar Beach Relaxation

Full day at leisure at your private villa. Infinity pool, private beach, spa treatments, and personal butler service. Everything is tailored to your preferences.

**Highlights:** Ultimate relaxation at a private beachfront villa.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Luxury Villa
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 9: Private Dhow Cruise & Snorkeling

Private dhow cruise along Zanzibar's stunning coastline. Snorkeling at pristine coral reefs. Fresh seafood lunch prepared on board. Sunset sailing with sparkling wine. Return to your villa for dinner.

**Highlights:** Private dhow cruise to exclusive snorkeling spots.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Luxury Villa
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 10: Private Spice Tour & Stone Town

Private guided spice tour on a historic plantation. Exclusive cooking class featuring Zanzibari cuisine. Afternoon private tour of Stone Town including the Sultan's Palace, Old Fort, and House of Wonders. Evening dinner at a private rooftop restaurant.

**Highlights:** Exclusive culinary and cultural immersion in Stone Town.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Luxury Villa
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 11: Mnemba Island Private Excursion

Full-day private excursion to Mnemba Island. Swim with sea turtles, snorkel on pristine reefs, and enjoy a gourmet picnic on a private sandbank. Your own personal paradise for the day.

**Highlights:** Private island day at Mnemba's exclusive marine reserve.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Luxury Villa
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 12: Full Day Spa & Beach

Day dedicated to wellness. In-villa spa treatments, yoga on the beach, and gourmet health-focused cuisine. Afternoon at leisure on your private beach.

**Highlights:** Wellness day with world-class spa treatments.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Luxury Villa
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 13: Jozani Forest & Farewell Dinner

Morning private tour of Jozani Forest, home to the Red Colobus monkey. Afternoon at leisure. Farewell gala dinner with private chef and live traditional music on the beach.

**Highlights:** Farewell gala dinner on the beach under the stars.

**Accommodation:** Zanzibar Luxury Villa
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 14: Departure

Private transfer to Zanzibar International Airport. VIP departure assistance.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
