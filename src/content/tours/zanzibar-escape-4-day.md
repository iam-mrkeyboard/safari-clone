Escape to the spice island of Zanzibar for a rejuvenating beach getaway. This 4-day package combines pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and a visit to the famous Nakupenda Sandbank. Perfect for a short tropical retreat after a safari or as a standalone beach holiday.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival in Zanzibar – Stone Town Tour

Arrive at Zanzibar International Airport where your driver will meet you. Depending on your arrival time, enjoy a guided tour of Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Wander through narrow alleyways, visit the Old Fort, House of Wonders, and the Darajani Market. Learn about the island's rich spice trade and Swahili history. Evening transfer to your beach resort.

**Highlights:** Immersive Stone Town walking tour through centuries of history and culture.

**Accommodation:** Beach Resort  
**Meal Plan:** Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Nakupenda Sandbank Excursion

After breakfast, take a boat to Nakupenda Sandbank, a stunning white-sand bar that emerges from the turquoise ocean at low tide. Spend the day swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing on this pristine sandbank. Enjoy a freshly grilled seafood lunch on the beach. Return to your resort late afternoon.

**Highlights:** Pristine sandbank surrounded by crystal-clear waters, snorkeling and fresh seafood.

**Accommodation:** Beach Resort  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Spice Tour & Beach Relaxation

Morning spice plantation tour where you'll see, smell, and taste Zanzibar's famous spices including cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, and vanilla. Learn about traditional uses and medicinal properties. Afternoon free to relax on the beach, swim, or enjoy resort amenities.

**Highlights:** Sensory spice tour and a lazy afternoon on Zanzibar's beautiful beaches.

**Accommodation:** Beach Resort  
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast on the beach before transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

**Highlights:** A final taste of paradise before your journey home.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
