Indulge in pure relaxation on Zanzibar's stunning coastline. This 6-day beach retreat at Upendo Beach offers the perfect escape with white-sand beaches, turquoise waters, and world-class hospitality. Ideal for honeymooners, couples, or anyone seeking a serene tropical getaway with optional excursions to explore the island's treasures.

## Itinerary

### Day 1: Arrival – Upendo Beach Resort

Arrive at Zanzibar Airport and transfer to Upendo Beach Resort on the northeast coast. Settle into your beachfront accommodation. Spend the afternoon relaxing by the pool or taking your first stroll on the pristine beach. Welcome dinner at the resort.

**Highlights:** Arrival at your beachfront paradise on Zanzibar's coast.

**Accommodation:** Upendo Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Dinner, Breakfast

### Day 2: Beach Relaxation & Optional Snorkeling

A full day at leisure. Lounge on the white-sand beach, swim in the warm Indian Ocean, or join an optional snorkeling trip to nearby Mnemba Island marine reserve. The reef teems with tropical fish, turtles, and vibrant coral. Sundowners on the beach.

**Highlights:** Optional snorkeling at Mnemba Atoll or pure beach bliss.

**Accommodation:** Upendo Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 3: Spice Tour & Stone Town

Morning spice plantation tour exploring Zanzibar's fragrant spice farms. Afternoon guided tour of Stone Town, including the Old Fort, House of Wonders, and Darajani Market. Evening dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the harbor.

**Highlights:** Cultural immersion with spices and Stone Town history.

**Accommodation:** Upendo Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 4: Dolphin Tour & Kwale Island

Full-day excursion to the southern coast. Boat trip to spot dolphins in their natural habitat. Visit Kwale Island with its pristine sandbanks and mangrove forests. Enjoy freshly caught seafood grilled on the beach. Return to the resort for evening relaxation.

**Highlights:** Dolphin spotting and an island castaway experience.

**Accommodation:** Upendo Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 5: Full Day at Leisure

Your final full day to enjoy everything Upendo Beach offers. Spa treatments, swimming, beach volleyball, or simply reading under a palm tree. Sunset dhow cruise with sparkling wine and canapes.

**Highlights:** Complete relaxation with a sunset dhow cruise finale.

**Accommodation:** Upendo Beach Resort
**Meal Plan:** Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

### Day 6: Departure

Farewell breakfast. Transfer to Zanzibar International Airport for departure.

**Meal Plan:** Breakfast
